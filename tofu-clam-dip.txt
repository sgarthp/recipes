Ingredients:
 - 1 block firm tofu, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
 - 1 can red clams
 - 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
 - 2 teaspoons soy sauce
 - 3 green onions, sliced fine

Directions:
 1. Mix all ingredients, gently, in a bowl to combine.

 2. (optional) Refridgerate for 2-4 hours to let flavors do their thing.

 3. Enjoy on crackers.
